Common Questions

Things people often wonder

Is there a cost to attend?

There is no charge to attend. The Altisima Winery event includes a complimentary wine tasting; all events include light refreshments. There is absolutely no obligation to retain our firm.

Can I bring my spouse or partner?

Please do — we warmly encourage it. Estate planning affects the whole household, and couples often find the conversation most valuable when they're together. Just register both names so we can save two seats.

Do I need to prepare anything beforehand?

Not at all. Simply bring yourself and any questions you've been carrying. We'll provide a brief pre-event questionnaire if you'd like to think ahead — but it's entirely optional.

What topics are covered?

Every session covers core estate planning: wills, revocable living trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, beneficiary designations, and more. Expand the "See Topics" section on either event card for the full list.

What happens after the event?

Deanna stays after each event for informal conversation — no appointment needed. If you'd like to go deeper, you can book a private follow-up to talk through your family's specific situation. Completely at your discretion, always.
